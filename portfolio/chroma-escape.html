Chroma Escape

About the Project

Concept

You get trapped inside a game. Your objective is to get to the light and escape the game. In order to do that, you will need to absorb the RGB colors to go through the corrosponding walls. By picking up 2 colors at the same time, you can mix the RGB colors to go through advanced walls.

Details

Engine: Unreal Engine
Genre: First Person Puzzle
Platform: PC (Windows)

This game is a work in progress, so it's not available to download.
