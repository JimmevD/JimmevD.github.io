Demonic Treasure

About the Project

Concept

This is a side-scroller platformer where the player uses the character’s powers to switch between normal and demon states. Using this power the player has to navigate through, over and under obstacles to find their way to the treasure chest.

Details

Engine: Unreal Engine
Genre: Side scroller platformer
Platform: PC (Windows)
