Knon Room

About the Project

Concept

Knon Room is a 2D bullet hell game where you are stuck in a box. You need to survive as long as possible and kill enemies to get the highest score possible. To do this, you will have to kill enemies. Different enemies have different amount of health, so some are harder to kill. You can pick up power-ups to help you.

Details

Engine: Unity
Genre: 2D Bullet hell
Platform: PC (Windows)
