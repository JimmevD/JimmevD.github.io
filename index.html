About Me

Hi, I am Jimme van Duijnhoven, a Game Design student at Saxion University of Applied Sciences. I started by studying Game Development, where I learned how to program games. I was incredibly excited that I finally got to create my own games. Eventually, I realized that while I enjoyed programming, it didn't push my creativity enough. That's when I switched to Game Design, and I quickly discovered that I had made the right choice.

My technical background allows me to quickly prototype and implement my own ideas, making it easier to experiment, iterate, and communicate with programmers, artists, and fellow designers. Although level design is where my passion lies, I enjoy being a versatile designer who can contribute throughout development. Whether I'm designing gameplay systems, building prototypes, or creating design documents, I'm always looking for ways to improve the player experience.
