Demonic Treasure

Demonic Treasure is a side scroller platformer where you play as a greedy half demon who has found a treasure map. He is now moving through the world in order to get to this chest. At the same time he is collecting as many coins as he can. Using his powers he can switch between the normal and demon state in an instance. While he is in different states the objects around him change and he uses this to maneuver through the levels.
